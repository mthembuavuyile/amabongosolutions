Two Ways to Recycle With Us

Whether you're a household dropping off a bag of bottles or a business generating tonnes of glass monthly — we have a solution as a top crushed bottles recycling company.

Bring your glass directly to our Mkondeni facility. No prior arrangement needed — just arrive, we weigh, and we pay. Suitable for households and small businesses.

  • All glass types accepted
  • Instant cash payment
  • Open Mon–Sat

Commercial Collection

We dispatch our fleet to collect from your business premises. Ideal for restaurants, hotels, distributors, bottle stores, and manufacturers with regular output.

  • Scheduled collections
  • Large-volume pickups
  • Contracted service agreements
  • Serving greater KZN Midlands

Who We Collect From

Any business generating glass waste qualifies for our commercial collection service. The more consistent your volume, the better the arrangement we can offer.

Restaurants & Hotels

Regular weekly or bi-weekly pickups for food service businesses and guest houses generating bottle and jar waste, perfectly aligned to your housekeeping cycle.

Bottle Stores

High-volume amber and green glass generators. We handle large loads efficiently directly from your premises.

Food & Bev Manufacturers

Contracted bulk collection for production waste, cullet, and off-spec glass from manufacturing and bottling lines.

Distributors & Offices

We handle breakage, slow-moving glass stock, and service centralised glass collection points for commercial office parks.
